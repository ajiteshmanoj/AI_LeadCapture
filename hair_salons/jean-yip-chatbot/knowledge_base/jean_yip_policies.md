# Jean Yip Group — Policies

## Deposit Policy

A **30% deposit** is required for all services where:
- The total service price exceeds **$100**, OR
- The service duration exceeds **2 hours**

This applies to: all colour services (full colour, balayage, highlights, ombre, toner), all chemical treatments (keratin, digital perm, cold perm, rebonding, Brazilian blowout), and combined service packages.

**How deposits work:**
- The deposit is collected before your appointment is confirmed (or on the day for walk-ins).
- It is deducted in full from your final bill.
- Payment accepted via PayNow, credit/debit card, or cash.

**Deposit forfeit:**
- If you cancel or reschedule with less than 24 hours' notice, the deposit is forfeited.
- In genuine emergencies, contact us as early as possible — we will do our best to accommodate.

---

## Cancellation Policy

- Cancel or reschedule at least **24 hours before** your appointment at no charge.
- Less than 24 hours' notice: deposit (if any) is forfeited; no-deposit bookings are recorded as a no-show.
- **Repeat no-shows** may require a deposit for all future bookings regardless of service price.

To cancel or reschedule: reply to this chat, call the salon directly, or contact us via WhatsApp.

---

## Rescheduling

- Rescheduling is free with 24+ hours' notice.
- Your original deposit carries over to the new appointment date.
- You may reschedule up to **2 times** per booking; a third change may require re-booking.

---

## Walk-In Policy

Walk-ins are welcome at all Jean Yip outlets, subject to stylist availability. During peak hours (weekends, public holiday eves, 12–2pm), wait times can be 30–60 minutes. Booking in advance is recommended for:
- Any colour or chemical service
- Principal/Creative Director stylists
- Weekend and school holiday slots

---

## Loyalty Programme — Beauté Card

Jean Yip's **Beauté Card** is a stored-value and points loyalty card:

- **Points** are earned on every dollar spent (rate varies by current campaign).
- Points are redeemable for services and products at all Jean Yip outlets.
- **Stored value** can be loaded and used across all outlets.
- Cards are available at any outlet or via the Jean Yip app.

There is no expiry on Beauté Card points as long as the card is used at least once every 12 months.

---

## Colour Allergy / Patch Test Policy

- Clients with known colour allergies or sensitive skin should inform us at the time of booking.
- A patch test is performed 48 hours before the appointment for clients flagging allergies or for first-time colour clients.
- Jean Yip reserves the right to decline service if a patch test shows an adverse reaction.

---

## Child Policy

- Children under 12 are welcome with a parent or guardian.
- Children's haircuts are available at all outlets (from $28).
- Quiet hours (Mon–Fri before noon) are recommended for children with sensory sensitivities.

---

## Pricing Transparency

- All prices quoted are "from" prices. Final pricing is confirmed by the stylist before any service begins.
- There are no hidden charges. Additional treatments (e.g. Olaplex, deep conditioning) are always opt-in and quoted separately.
- Price differences across outlets are minimal but may exist based on outlet operating costs.
