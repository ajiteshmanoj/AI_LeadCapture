# Jean Yip Group — Brand Overview

## History

Jean Yip Group was founded in 1982 by Madam Jean Yip, a pioneering Singapore hairdresser who built one of Southeast Asia's most recognised salon brands from a single outlet. Over four decades the company has grown into Singapore's largest homegrown salon chain, operating more than 60 outlets island-wide across major shopping malls, MRT-connected hubs, and residential estates.

## Scale

- 60+ outlets across Singapore (as of 2025)
- 800+ trained stylists and beauty professionals
- Presence in Orchard Road, the East, West, North, and Central regions
- Serving hundreds of thousands of clients annually

## Brand Philosophy

Jean Yip's philosophy centres on accessible, professional hair care for every Singaporean — from a simple trim to editorial colour transformations. The brand is known for:

- Consistent quality across all outlets driven by structured stylist training programmes
- A clear stylist tier system (Junior, Stylist, Senior, Principal, Creative Director) that helps clients choose the expertise level that suits their budget and service complexity
- Ongoing investment in stylist education, including international training partnerships

## Awards and Recognition

- Multiple *Her World* and *Female* magazine "Best Salon" nominations and wins
- Recognised as a Singapore home-grown brand by Enterprise Singapore
- Regular feature in Singapore lifestyle and beauty media as a trusted salon institution

## Services Offered

Jean Yip provides a comprehensive range of hair and beauty services. At select outlets, nail and facial services are also available. Full service menu: see `jean_yip_services.md`.

## Digital and Loyalty

Jean Yip operates the **Beauté Card** loyalty programme, allowing clients to accumulate points redeemable for services and products. Digital booking is available via chatbot, phone, and walk-in.
