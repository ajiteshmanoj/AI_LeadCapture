# Jean Yip Group — Stylist Tiers and Requesting a Stylist

## Stylist Tier System

Jean Yip uses a five-tier system to classify stylists by experience, training, and specialisation. Higher tiers command higher prices and typically require more advance booking.

| Tier | Typical Experience | Pricing Relative to Base | Notes |
|------|--------------------|--------------------------|-------|
| **Junior** | 0–2 years | Base price | Supervised; great for simple cuts and root touch-ups |
| **Stylist** | 2–4 years | +15–25% | Competent across core services |
| **Senior** | 4–8 years | +40–60% | The most common tier for colour and chemical services |
| **Principal** | 8–12 years | +80–120% | Colour specialists, editorial, bridal; limited availability |
| **Creative Director** | 12+ years | +140–200% | Top of the house; editorial, award work; book far in advance |

Prices shown in `jean_yip_services.md` are Senior Stylist base rates unless otherwise stated.

## How to Request a Specific Stylist

Clients can request a specific stylist when booking:

1. Tell us the stylist's name when booking via chat, phone, or walk-in.
2. We will check that stylist's availability for your preferred date, time, and outlet.
3. If your preferred stylist is unavailable, we can suggest an alternative of the same or adjacent tier, or put you on their next available slot.

**Important notes:**
- Principal and Creative Director stylists book out 1–3 weeks in advance, especially for weekends. Book early.
- If no preference is given, we assign the best-available stylist for your service type.
- Stylist availability is outlet-specific — a stylist at Tampines Mall cannot be booked at Jurong Point.

## Tips for First-Time Clients

- Bring reference photos for colour and style inspiration.
- For colour services, arrive with dry, unwashed hair (no conditioner same day).
- Disclose any recent chemical treatments (box dye, keratin, relaxers) so the stylist can plan appropriately.
- If you have colour allergies, flag them at booking — our stylists will do a patch test 48 hours before chemical services if needed.
