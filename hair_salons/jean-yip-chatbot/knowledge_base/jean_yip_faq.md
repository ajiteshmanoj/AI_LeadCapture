# Jean Yip Group — Frequently Asked Questions

---

**Q1: How do I book an appointment?**
A: You can book directly via this chat — just tell Fiona what service you need and your preferred outlet. You can also call any Jean Yip salon directly or walk in (subject to availability).

---

**Q2: Do I need to pay a deposit?**
A: A 30% deposit is required for colour, chemical, and treatment services priced above $100 or lasting more than 2 hours. For haircuts and blow-dries, no deposit is needed. The deposit is deducted from your final bill on the day.

---

**Q3: Can I request a specific stylist?**
A: Yes. Let us know the stylist's name when booking and we'll check their availability. Principal and Creative Director stylists are popular — book 1–2 weeks ahead for weekends.

---

**Q4: What is the cancellation policy?**
A: Cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours before your appointment at no charge. Cancellations with less than 24 hours' notice forfeit the deposit.

---

**Q5: How much does a haircut cost at Jean Yip?**
A: Haircuts start from $38 (Junior Stylist, short hair) and go up to $120+ (Creative Director, long hair). A standard Haircut & Blow Dry with a Senior Stylist for short-to-medium hair is around $68. Final price is confirmed before we start.

---

**Q6: How much does balayage cost?**
A: Balayage starts from $180 for short hair with a Senior Stylist. For long hair or Principal Stylist service, expect $250–$310+. A 30% deposit is required as this service exceeds $100 and 2 hours.

---

**Q7: How long does a keratin treatment take?**
A: Typically 2–3 hours depending on hair length and thickness. You'll need to keep your hair dry and avoid tying it for 72 hours after the treatment.

---

**Q8: Can I walk in without an appointment?**
A: Yes, walk-ins are welcome. However, for colour, chemical, or treatment services — and for weekends — we strongly recommend booking in advance. Wait times on weekends can be 30–60 minutes.

---

**Q9: What is the Beauté Card loyalty programme?**
A: The Beauté Card earns you points on every dollar spent, redeemable for services and products across all Jean Yip outlets. Cards are available at any outlet. Points don't expire as long as the card is used once a year.

---

**Q10: Do you do patch tests for colour?**
A: Yes. If you have colour allergies or are a first-time colour client, we'll do a patch test 48 hours before your appointment. Please flag this at the time of booking.

---

**Q11: What outlets are near Jurong?**
A: Jean Yip @ Jurong Point is our West-side flagship — directly connected to Boon Lay MRT (EW27). We also have outlets at Westgate and IMM. Call +65 6790 0001 for the full West outlet list.

---

**Q12: How long does a full colour take?**
A: Full colour for short hair takes about 60–90 minutes. Medium hair runs 90–120 minutes. For long or thick hair, allow 2+ hours. Always book some buffer time.

---

**Q13: Can I bring my child to the salon?**
A: Yes, children are welcome. We offer kids' haircuts from $28. Weekday mornings are quieter and more comfortable for young children.

---

**Q14: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: Cash, credit/debit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayNow, and Beauté Card stored value. We do not accept NETS Flash.

---

**Q15: How do I reschedule my appointment?**
A: Reply here to reschedule, or call the salon directly. Rescheduling is free with 24+ hours' notice and your deposit carries over to the new date.

---

**Q16: Is parking available at your outlets?**
A: All three seeded outlets (Jurong Point, Tampines Mall, Bugis Junction) have mall carparks. We recommend arriving by MRT on weekends as carparks fill quickly. See `jean_yip_salons.md` for specifics.

---

**Q17: Do you offer bridal hair services?**
A: Yes. Our Principal and Creative Director stylists have extensive bridal experience. Bridal packages (including trial and day-of styling) are available — ask Fiona to connect you with the relevant stylist at your preferred outlet.

---

**Q18: My hair was coloured elsewhere. Can you still help?**
A: Yes, but please let your stylist know during consultation. Prior chemical treatments affect what we can safely do next. The stylist may recommend a strand test first.

---

**Q19: What should I wear for a colour appointment?**
A: Wear an old or dark top — we provide capes but staining can occasionally happen. Arrive with dry, unwashed hair (skip conditioner on wash day).

---

**Q20: How do I find my nearest Jean Yip outlet?**
A: We have 60+ outlets island-wide. Tell Fiona your area or nearest MRT and she'll point you to the closest outlet. You can also call our main line at +65 6790 0001.
