## General Information

- **Reservations:** Accepted at all outlets for groups of 5 or more. Groups under 5 are walk-in and join the physical or digital queue.
- **Queue System:** Most outlets use a digital queue system. Guests can scan a QR code at the outlet to join the queue remotely.
- **Peak Hours:** Weekday lunch 12–1:30pm and dinner 7–9pm. Weekend lunch from 11:30am and dinner from 6:30pm tend to be busiest. Expect 20–45 minute waits during peak hours.
- **Contactless Payment:** All major cards, PayNow, GrabPay, and Apple/Google Pay accepted.
- **Private Dining:** Available at Paragon only. Minimum 10 pax, maximum 20 pax. Refundable deposit of $50 per table required.
