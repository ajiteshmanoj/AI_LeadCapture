# Din Tai Fung Singapore — Menu

## IMPORTANT DIETARY NOTICE

**Din Tai Fung Singapore is NOT Halal-certified.** Our kitchen uses pork products, including lard in some doughs. We do not hold any Halal certification from MUIS or any other certifying body. We are unable to make any Halal guarantees for any dish. Guests who require Halal-certified food should be advised accordingly.

Pork items are marked **(PORK)** below. Vegetarian items are marked **(VEG)**. Note that all items are cooked in a shared kitchen — cross-contamination with pork and other allergens cannot be ruled out.
