# Din Tai Fung Singapore — Reservations Policy

## Who Can Make Reservations?

Reservations are accepted for **groups of 5 or more guests** at all outlets except Changi Airport Terminal 3.

Groups of 4 or fewer are welcome to **walk in** and join our queue system. At most outlets, guests can scan a QR code at the entrance to receive a queue number and wait nearby.

---

## How to Reserve

1. **Via chatbot (Mei):** Chat with our reservation assistant on our website. Mei can check availability and confirm your reservation in real time.
2. **By phone:** Call the specific outlet directly. Numbers are listed on our website and in the Outlets & Hours document.
3. **Walk-in:** Join the in-store queue. Groups of 5+ may still be accommodated as walk-ins subject to availability.

---

## Reservation Lead Time

- **Same-day reservations:** Available subject to slot availability — contact the outlet or use the chatbot.
- **Advance bookings:** Recommended for weekends, public holidays, and groups of 10+.
- **Private Dining Room (Paragon):** Book at least 3 days in advance for groups of 10–20.

---

## Walk-In & Queue Policy

- Walk-in guests are added to a physical or digital queue.
- Queue numbers are held for **10 minutes** from notification. If your party is not ready, you may lose your position.
- Estimated wait times are provided at the queue counter and via the digital system.
- Typical peak-hour waits: 20–45 minutes at ION Orchard and Paragon on weekends.

---

## Private Dining Room (Paragon Only)

- **Capacity:** 10–20 guests
- **Deposit:** $50 (refundable) per private dining room table, payable at time of booking
- **Menu:** Standard a la carte menu; set menu options available on request
- **Booking lead time:** Minimum 3 business days in advance
- **Cancellation:** Deposit refunded if cancelled 48 hours or more before the reservation. Forfeited for no-shows or cancellations within 48 hours.
- Contact: +65 6836 8336 or reservations@dintaifung.com.sg

---

## Large Group & Catering Enquiries

For groups of **21 pax or more**, or for **off-site catering** (corporate events, weddings, parties), please contact our events team:

- **Email:** reservations@dintaifung.com.sg
- **Phone:** +65 6836 8336

Our events team can discuss:
- Custom menus and set banquet options
- Minimum spend requirements
- Venue selection and logistics
- Pricing and payment terms

Please allow 1–2 business days for a response.

---

## Cancellation Policy (Standard Reservations)

- No penalty for cancellations or amendments with at least **2 hours' notice**.
- For groups of 8+, we appreciate **24 hours' notice** to release the table for other guests.
- Repeated no-shows may result in bookings being declined.

---

## Special Occasions

Let us know if you are celebrating a birthday, anniversary, or other occasion. Our team will do their best to note this for your visit. Din Tai Fung does not provide cake-cutting services or outside food arrangements — please check with individual outlets.

---

## Dietary & Accessibility

- **Dietary:** Please inform us of dietary restrictions or allergies when booking. Note that our kitchen is not Halal-certified and uses pork. We cannot guarantee allergen-free preparation.
- **Wheelchair access:** All main mall outlets are wheelchair-accessible. Please request a ground-floor or accessible table when booking.
- **High chairs:** Available on request at all outlets.

---

## Payment at the Restaurant

- Payment is due at the end of the meal.
- Accepted: Visa, Mastercard, Amex, JCB, PayNow, GrabPay, Apple Pay, Google Pay, cash.
- A 10% service charge and prevailing GST apply to all bills.
- Deposits (for private dining) are deducted from the final bill.
