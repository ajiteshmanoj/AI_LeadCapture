# Huttons Asia — Company Overview

## About Huttons

Huttons Asia Pte Ltd was established in **2002** and has grown to become Singapore's **largest private real estate agency**. Huttons is an independently owned agency (not a franchise), headquartered at 3 Bishan Place, #05-01 CPF Bishan Building, Singapore 579838.

**Key facts:**
- Over **5,300 active real estate agents** (salespersons) across Singapore
- Licensed by the **Council for Estate Agencies (CEA)** — all Huttons agents hold valid CEA registration
- Ranked #1 private agency by agent headcount (multiple consecutive years)
- Serves buyers, sellers, landlords, and tenants across all property segments

## Divisions

### Residential
Handles HDB resale, private condominium resale, and landed properties. Specialists by district allow buyers to get hyper-local expertise (e.g. D20 Bishan/Thomson specialist, D18 Tampines specialist).

### New Launches
Dedicated new launch division handling developer launch events, preview registrations, and buyer representation for new development projects across Singapore. Huttons is an appointed marketing agent for many Singapore developers.

### Commercial & Industrial
Covers shophouses, retail units, office spaces, industrial properties (B1/B2), strata industrial units, and commercial investments. Specialists advise on PSF, rental yield, and zoning.

### International
Huttons International assists Singapore buyers and investors looking at overseas markets including UK, Australia, Japan, and other Southeast Asian markets.

### Project Marketing
Manages en-bloc sales, collective sales, and large-scale developer projects.
