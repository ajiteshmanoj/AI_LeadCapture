## Stamp Duties

### Buyer's Stamp Duty (BSD) — Payable by All Buyers

Calculated on purchase price or market value (whichever is higher):

| Purchase Price | BSD Rate |
|----------------|----------|
| First $180,000 | 1% |
| Next $180,000 | 2% |
| Next $640,000 | 3% |
| Next $500,000 | 4% |
| Next $1,500,000 | 5% |
| Amount exceeding $3,000,000 | 6% |

**Example**: $1.5M condo:
- 1% on $180K = $1,800
- 2% on $180K = $3,600
- 3% on $640K = $19,200
- 4% on $500K = $20,000
- Total BSD = $44,600

### Additional Buyer's Stamp Duty (ABSD)

| Profile | 1st Property | 2nd Property | 3rd+ Property |
|---------|-------------|--------------|---------------|
| Singapore Citizen | 0% | 20% | 30% |
| Permanent Resident | 5% | 30% | 35% |
| Foreigner | 60% | 60% | 60% |
| Entity (Company) | 65% | 65% | 65% |

**Critical for foreigners**: On a $1.5M condo, a foreigner pays 60% ABSD = $900,000. Total cost = $1.5M + $44.6K BSD + $900K ABSD = ~$2.44M. Always discuss citizenship status before viewing.

## Option to Purchase (OTP) Process

### Step 1: Negotiate & Grant OTP
- Seller grants OTP to buyer after price agreement
- Buyer pays **1% option fee** (cash) to seller
- OTP is valid for **21 days** (can be extended by mutual agreement)

### Step 2: Exercise OTP (within 21 days)
- Buyer pays **4% exercise fee** (cash or CPF), totalling 5% of purchase price
- Appoint a conveyancing lawyer immediately if not already done
- Lawyer handles due diligence: title search, caveats, outstanding mortgages

### Step 3: Completion (10–12 weeks from OTP exercise)
- Legal completion timeline: typically 8–10 weeks after OTP exercise
- Remaining purchase price paid at completion
- Mortgage disbursed by bank at completion
- Keys handed over

## Financing Private Property

### Loan-to-Value (LTV) Limits
| Loan | 1st Property | 2nd Property | 3rd+ |
|------|-------------|--------------|------|
| Bank (no outstanding loans) | 75% | 45% | 35% |
| HDB loan | Not applicable | Not applicable | Not applicable |

### Total Debt Servicing Ratio (TDSR)
- Maximum 55% of gross monthly income can go toward all debt servicing
- Includes car loans, credit card, personal loans, existing mortgages

### Mortgage Stress Test
- Banks assess at a stressed interest rate (currently ~4.0%–4.5%)
- Huttons agents can refer buyers to mortgage brokers for pre-approval

### CPF Usage for Private Property
- SC and PRs can use CPF OA for private property purchase
- Subject to the property's remaining lease (must cover buyer to age 95)
- Withdrawal Limit (WL): CPF can cover up to 120% of Valuation Limit for private property

## Private Property Due Diligence Checklist

1. **Title search**: No encumbrances, mortgages discharged
2. **Development charge**: For land use intensification
3. **Outstanding conservancy/sinking fund**: Seller's responsibility to clear
4. **Defects inspection**: Commission a home inspector for older properties
5. **Check lease term**: Freehold vs leasehold; CPF withdrawal limits for leasehold
6. **En-bloc risk**: Check if development has voted or applied for en-bloc sale
7. **Rental history**: For investment — check historical rents (PropertyGuru, SRX)
8. **Planning approvals**: URA master plan for surrounding area
