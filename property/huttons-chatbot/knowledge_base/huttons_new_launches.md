## Purchasing a New Launch — Process

1. **Register interest** with Huttons agent (no commitment)
2. **Attend preview/show flat** — view actual units and finishes
3. **Balloting** (for popular launches) or direct selection
4. **Exercise Option to Purchase (OTP)**: pay 5% booking fee (cash)
5. **Sign Sale & Purchase Agreement (SPA)**: within 8 weeks of OTP
6. **Progressive payments**: follow developer's payment schedule
7. **TOP (Temporary Occupation Permit)**: keys collection; balance payable
8. **CSC (Certificate of Statutory Completion)**: full legal completion

**Note**: No stamp duty for first-time SC buyers on first residential property. PRs and foreigners pay ABSD. Our agents calculate your total costs before commitment.

## CPF Usage for New Launches
- Singapore Citizens and PRs can use CPF Ordinary Account (OA) for down payment and monthly mortgage installments
- CPF usage subject to Valuation Limit (VL) and Withdrawal Limit (WL)
- Foreigners cannot use CPF

## Loan & LTV
- Maximum 75% LTV (Loan-to-Value) for first residential property (for SC/PR)
- Second property: max 45% LTV
- Interest rate (indicative): 3.5%–4.5% p.a. (floating or fixed packages vary)
- Huttons partners with major banks — agents can facilitate mortgage referrals
