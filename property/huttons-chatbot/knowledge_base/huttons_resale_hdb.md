# Huttons Asia — HDB Resale Guide

## Eligibility to Buy HDB Resale

### Singapore Citizens (SC)
- Can buy any HDB resale flat (1-room to 5-room, Executive, DBSS, EA, EM)
- Must meet family nucleus requirement (single SC above 35 can buy 2-room or smaller)
- Income ceiling: For CPF Housing Grants, family income must not exceed $14,000/month
- No ownership of private property (if buying with grants)

### Permanent Residents (PR)
- PRs can buy HDB resale with an eligible family nucleus (e.g. married to SC or another PR)
- PRs must have obtained PR status for at least 3 years before they can buy HDB resale
- Cannot own other HDB flats
- PRs are NOT eligible for CPF Housing Grants

### Foreigners
- **Foreigners CANNOT buy HDB flats** (resale or BTO). This is a firm rule.
- Foreigners may only buy private property (condos, landed with restrictions) or commercial/industrial properties.

## HDB Resale Process (8–12 Weeks Timeline)

### Step 1: HFE Letter (HDB Flat Eligibility)
- Apply online via HDB's MyFlat portal
- Processing: 30 working days
- HFE letter confirms eligibility, CPF grant amount, and HDB loan eligibility
- **You cannot make an offer without a valid HFE letter**

### Step 2: Find a Flat & Negotiate
- Search on HDB Resale Portal, PropertyGuru, 99.co
- Negotiate price with seller/seller's agent
- Key metric: Check HDB's recent transacted prices (HDB Resale Price Index)
- Check for Cash Over Valuation (COV) — cash amount above HDB's valuation if seller asks for more

### Step 3: Option to Purchase (OTP)
- Pay 1% option fee (cash, max $5,000) to seller
- 21-day option period to decide
- HDB conducts valuation during this period
- If you don't exercise: forfeit 25% of option fee

### Step 4: Exercise OTP
- Pay 4% exercise fee (cash or CPF, minus the 1% already paid)
- Submit intent to resale on HDB portal within 7 days

### Step 5: HDB Resale Application
- Both buyer and seller submit applications via HDB portal
- HDB verifies documents, confirms eligibility

### Step 6: HDB Appointment
- HDB issues a date for the resale completion appointment (typically 8 weeks after application)
- Buyer and seller attend HDB appointment
- Keys exchanged, final payment made

### Step 7: Key Collection
- Collect keys, verify flat condition
- Arrange utilities (SP Group), broadband, etc.

## CPF Housing Grants (for SC Buyers)

### Enhanced CPF Housing Grant (EHG)
- For first-time buyers with monthly income ≤ $9,000
- Up to $120,000 grant depending on income bracket
- Applicable to HDB BTO and resale

### Proximity Housing Grant (PHG)
- $30,000 if buying within 4km of parents' home
- $20,000 if parents/children move within 4km of you

### Family Grant
- $80,000 for SC+SC couples buying 2-room to 4-room
- $40,000 for SC+PR couples

## Financing HDB Resale

### HDB Concessionary Loan
- Interest rate: 2.6% p.a. (CPF OA interest + 0.1%)
- Max 80% LTV (if eligible)
- First-timer SC buyers eligible if monthly income ≤ $14,000
- Requires HFE letter showing HDB loan eligibility

### Bank Loan
- Typically lower initial rate but subject to market fluctuation
- Max 75% LTV
- TDSR (Total Debt Servicing Ratio) applies: max 55% of gross monthly income

## Key Things to Check When Viewing an HDB

1. **Remaining lease**: Ideally 60+ years for CPF usage at full valuation
2. **Structural condition**: Cracks, seepage, water damage
3. **Facing**: North-South facing preferred (avoids afternoon sun)
4. **Floor level**: Higher = better ventilation, potentially higher price
5. **Surrounding amenities**: Schools, MRT, markets, hawker centres
6. **Renovation needed**: Factor into total budget
7. **Ethnic Integration Policy (EIP) quota**: Check you're eligible to buy in that block/precinct
