# Virgin Active Singapore — Overview

## Brand

Virgin Active is part of the Virgin Group, founded by Sir Richard Branson. The brand operates premium health clubs across the UK, South Africa, Italy, Australia, and Asia. Virgin Active is known for its full-service approach: state-of-the-art gym equipment, a broad class timetable, spa facilities, and a community-first culture.

Virgin Active entered Singapore as part of its Asian expansion strategy, positioning itself as the premium full-service gym for professionals and fitness enthusiasts who want more than just weights and treadmills.

## Singapore Presence

Virgin Active Singapore operates six clubs across the island, including:

- **Novena Square** — Thomson Road, Central Singapore. One of the flagship clubs, close to Novena MRT. Full facilities including group fitness studios, functional training zone, and café.
- **Raffles Place (Chevron House)** — Located in the heart of the CBD. Popular with professionals for lunchtime and after-work classes. Premium locker facilities.
- **VivoCity** — HarbourFront Walk. Largest club in Singapore with a 25-metre outdoor-style pool and aqua fitness classes. Ideal for families and weekend sessions.
- Three additional locations across the island (details available at member services).

## Facilities (varies by club)

- Free weights and resistance machines
- Functional training zones (ropes, sleds, racks)
- Cardio deck with panoramic views (select clubs)
- Group fitness studios with sprung floors
- Spin/cycling studios
- Yoga and Pilates studios
- 25-metre swimming pool (VivoCity)
- Spa and steam rooms (select clubs)
- Towel service (Premium members)
- Café and nutrition bar
- Secure lockers and changing rooms
- Premium lounge (Premium members)

## Mission

To inspire people to a lifetime of fitness by creating the most welcoming, high-energy, and results-driven gym experience in Singapore.
