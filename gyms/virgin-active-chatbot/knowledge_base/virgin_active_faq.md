# Virgin Active Singapore — Frequently Asked Questions

## Membership

**Q: How much does a Virgin Active membership cost?**
A: We have three tiers: Classic at $99/month (all clubs and classes), Plus at $139/month (adds 1 guest pass/month and spa access), and Premium at $179/month (adds 2 guest passes, 1 PT session/month, and towel service). There is no joining fee for any tier.

**Q: Is there a lock-in contract?**
A: No lock-in contract. All memberships are month-to-month. You can cancel with 30 days' written notice.

**Q: Can I try before joining?**
A: Yes. We offer a complimentary trial class for first-time visitors. Chat with Alex or call any club to book your free trial class — no membership required.

**Q: How do I cancel my membership?**
A: Email singapore@virginactive.com.sg with your name, NRIC/FIN, and reason for cancellation. Give 30 days' notice before your next billing date. No cancellation fees apply.

**Q: Can I freeze my membership?**
A: Yes. Membership freeze is $20/month. Minimum 1 month, maximum 3 months per year. Email your home club or singapore@virginactive.com.sg to request a freeze — the chatbot cannot process this.

**Q: Can I upgrade or downgrade my tier?**
A: Yes, at any time. Changes take effect at the start of the next billing cycle. Contact Member Services or speak to reception.

**Q: Is there a joining fee?**
A: No — there is no joining fee for any membership tier at Virgin Active Singapore.

## Classes

**Q: Do I need to book classes in advance?**
A: Yes. Class booking is required to guarantee your spot. We recommend booking at least 24 hours ahead. Popular classes like HIIT Blast and Spinning fill up fast.

**Q: How do I book a class?**
A: You can book via our chatbot (Alex), the Virgin Active mobile app, or by calling your preferred club directly.

**Q: What is the cancellation policy for classes?**
A: Cancel up to 2 hours before the class starts without penalty. Late cancellations or no-shows count as a strike. Three strikes in a month may temporarily limit your booking access.

**Q: Can I just walk in to a class without booking?**
A: Only if there are spare spots at the door — but most popular classes are fully booked. We strongly recommend booking in advance to avoid disappointment.

**Q: Are classes included in all memberships?**
A: Yes. Unlimited fitness classes are included in all three membership tiers (Classic, Plus, and Premium).

**Q: What should I bring to a class?**
A: Generally: comfortable activewear, sports shoes, a water bottle, and a towel. For specific classes:
- Yoga/Hot Yoga: mat (borrowable), small towel, water
- Spinning: cycling shoes (rentable), padded shorts optional
- Reformer Pilates: grip socks (required, available to buy at reception)
- Boxing Cardio: hand wraps (available at the club)
- Aqua Aerobics: swimwear, towel

**Q: Are there beginner-friendly classes?**
A: Yes. Boxing Cardio, Aqua Aerobics, Zumba, Mat Pilates, Hot Yoga, and Dance Cardio are all suitable for beginners. Our instructors always offer modifications.

## Clubs & Facilities

**Q: Which clubs have a swimming pool?**
A: Our VivoCity club has a 25-metre outdoor-style swimming pool. Aqua Aerobics and Aqua Zumba classes are held there. Other clubs do not currently have pools.

**Q: Can I visit any club with my membership?**
A: Yes. All membership tiers include access to all Virgin Active Singapore clubs.

**Q: What are the opening hours?**
A: Hours vary by club. Generally: weekdays 6am–10/11pm, Saturdays 7am–8/9pm, Sundays 8am–6/8pm. Check with your specific club or ask Alex for exact hours.

**Q: Is there parking available?**
A: Yes at most clubs. Novena Square has a carpark with 30-minute validation for members. VivoCity has HarbourFront Centre carpark nearby. Raffles Place is CBD — we recommend MRT.

**Q: Do you have personal trainers?**
A: Yes. Certified PTs are available at all clubs. For personalised fitness or nutrition plans, booking a PT session is the best option. Alex (the chatbot) cannot give fitness or nutrition advice — that's what our PTs are for.

## Guest Passes & Family

**Q: Can I bring a friend to the gym?**
A: Plus ($139/month) members get 1 guest pass/month. Premium ($179/month) members get 2. Guests must be accompanied by the member at all times. Guest passes cannot be accumulated or transferred.

**Q: Is there a family membership?**
A: Family add-on rates are available. Contact Member Services for current pricing.

## Other

**Q: Is there a Virgin Active mobile app?**
A: Yes. The Virgin Active app lets you book classes, view timetables, track workouts, and manage your membership. Available on iOS and Android.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: GIRO/Direct Debit and credit cards (Visa, Mastercard). Setup at the club during sign-up.

**Q: I have a complaint. Who should I speak to?**
A: We're sorry to hear that. You can ask Alex to connect you with our Member Services team, or email singapore@virginactive.com.sg and a manager will be in touch within 1 business day.
