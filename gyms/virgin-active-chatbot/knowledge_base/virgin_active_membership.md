# Virgin Active Singapore — Membership

## Membership Tiers

There is **no joining fee** for any membership tier. You pay only the monthly fee.

### Classic — $99/month

**Best for:** Members who want full access to all clubs and classes at the best value.

**Included:**
- Access to all Virgin Active Singapore clubs
- Unlimited fitness classes (all types, all clubs)
- Full gym floor access (free weights, machines, functional zone)
- Locker room access with secure electronic lockers
- Standard changing facilities

---

### Plus — $139/month

**Best for:** Members who want to bring a guest occasionally or enjoy spa facilities.

**Included — everything in Classic, plus:**
- 1 guest pass per month (valid for one guest visit to any club)
- Spa and wellness access (steam room, sauna, jacuzzi where available)
- Priority class booking (book 48 hours before non-members, vs 24 hours)

---

### Premium — $179/month

**Best for:** Members who want the full Virgin Active experience with added perks.

**Included — everything in Plus, plus:**
- 2 guest passes per month
- 1 personal training session per month (30-minute session with a certified VA PT)
- Towel service (fresh towels provided at every visit — no need to bring your own)
- Exclusive premium lounge access (select clubs)

---

## Trial Class

New to Virgin Active? We offer a complimentary trial class before you commit to a membership. This is the best way to experience the club, meet our instructors, and decide if Virgin Active is right for you.

To book a trial class, just chat with Alex (our chatbot) or call any club directly.

---

## Membership Freeze

Members can freeze their membership for a fee of **$20/month**. Freeze requests must be submitted via email to your home club or to singapore@virginactive.com.sg. The chatbot cannot process freeze requests — please email us directly.

Minimum freeze duration: 1 month. Maximum: 3 months per year (policy subject to change).

---

## Membership Cancellation

To cancel your membership:
1. Email singapore@virginactive.com.sg with your full name, NRIC/FIN, and reason for cancellation.
2. Provide 30 days' written notice before your next billing date.
3. Your membership remains active for the notice period.

There are no cancellation fees.

---

## Corporate Membership

Virgin Active Singapore offers corporate membership rates for companies with 5 or more employees. Contact our corporate team at corporate.sg@virginactive.com.sg for a customised proposal.

---

## Family Membership

Family add-on rates are available. Contact Member Services for current pricing.

---

## Guest Passes

Guest passes are included with Plus ($139) and Premium ($179) memberships. Guests must be accompanied by the member at all times. Guest passes cannot be accumulated or transferred.

---

## Billing

- Membership is billed monthly on the date you joined.
- Payment is by GIRO/Direct Debit or credit card on file.
- No lock-in contract — month-to-month.

---

## Upgrading or Downgrading

You can change your membership tier at any time. Changes take effect at the start of the next billing cycle. Contact Member Services or speak to reception at any club.
