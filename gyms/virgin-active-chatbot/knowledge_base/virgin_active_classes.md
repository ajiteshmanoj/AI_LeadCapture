# Virgin Active Singapore — Fitness Classes

## Class Booking Policy

All classes require advance booking. Members can book via:
- The Virgin Active chatbot (Alex)
- The Virgin Active mobile app
- Calling your preferred club directly

We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance. Popular classes like HIIT Blast and Spinning fill up within hours of booking windows opening.

## Cancellation Policy

Cancel up to 2 hours before class start time without penalty. Late cancellations and no-shows count as strikes. Three strikes in a month may temporarily restrict booking access.

## Class Types

### Yoga

**Hot Yoga** (All levels)
Duration: 60 minutes
What to expect: A dynamic yoga flow practised in a heated room (37°C). Builds flexibility, strength, and mental focus. Suitable for complete beginners and experienced yogis alike.
What to bring: Mat (available to borrow), towel, water bottle, comfortable activewear.
Clubs: Novena

**Vinyasa Flow** (All levels)
Duration: 60 minutes
What to expect: A flowing sequence linking breath to movement. Great for building strength and flexibility progressively.
Clubs: Select locations — check the app for current schedule.

**Yin Yoga** (All levels)
Duration: 75 minutes
What to expect: Slow-paced, long-hold poses targeting deep connective tissue. Excellent complement to high-intensity training.

### HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training)

**HIIT Blast** (Intermediate)
Duration: 45 minutes
What to expect: Short, intense bursts of cardio and strength exercises with active recovery periods. Burns maximum calories in minimum time. Some prior fitness experience recommended.
What to bring: Sports shoes, water bottle, towel.
Clubs: Novena

**HIIT Circuit** (Beginner–Intermediate)
Duration: 45 minutes
What to expect: Station-based circuit combining cardio and resistance. Modifications offered for all fitness levels.

### Cycling / Spinning

**Spinning** (All levels)
Duration: 60 minutes
What to expect: Indoor cycling class with varied resistance and pace. Instructor-led rides simulate hills, sprints, and flat roads. Great cardio workout — no cycling experience needed.
What to bring: Sports shoes (cycling shoes available to rent), water bottle, towel.
Clubs: Raffles Place

### Boxing & Martial Arts

**Boxing Cardio** (Beginner)
Duration: 60 minutes
What to expect: Non-contact boxing techniques combined with cardio intervals. Learn jabs, crosses, hooks and uppercuts while getting a full-body workout. No boxing experience required.
What to bring: Hand wraps (available at the club), sports shoes, water bottle.
Clubs: Raffles Place

**Muay Thai Cardio** (All levels)
Duration: 60 minutes
What to expect: High-energy class combining Muay Thai strikes (punches, kicks, knees, elbows) with cardio conditioning. Non-contact.

### Aqua Fitness

**Aqua Aerobics** (All levels)
Duration: 60 minutes
What to expect: Low-impact aerobic exercise in the pool. Gentle on joints, great for rehabilitation, older adults, or anyone wanting a refreshing workout alternative to the gym floor.
What to bring: Swimwear, towel, goggles (optional).
Clubs: VivoCity (25-metre pool)

**Aqua Zumba** (All levels)
Duration: 45 minutes
What to expect: Latin dance moves adapted for the water. Fun, energetic, low impact.

### Pilates

**Reformer Pilates** (All levels)
Duration: 50 minutes
What to expect: Core-focused workout using the Pilates Reformer machine. Builds posture, flexibility, and deep stabilising muscles. Class size is small (typically 8–10 per session).
What to bring: Grip socks (required, available for purchase at reception), comfortable clothing.

**Mat Pilates** (All levels)
Duration: 45 minutes
What to expect: Foundational Pilates exercises performed on a mat. No equipment required. Great starting point before progressing to Reformer.

### Dance Fitness

**Zumba** (All levels)
Duration: 60 minutes
What to expect: Latin-inspired dance fitness class that doesn't feel like a workout. High energy, no dance experience needed.

**Dance Cardio** (All levels)
Duration: 45 minutes
What to expect: Choreographed routines set to current music. Easy to follow, maximum fun.

## Personal Training

Personal Training sessions are available at all clubs. PT is recommended for:
- Members new to strength training
- Goal-specific programming (weight loss, muscle gain, marathon prep)
- Recovering from injury
- Members who want personalised fitness or nutrition plans

Note: Alex (the chatbot) cannot give fitness or nutrition advice — for personalised guidance, please speak to one of our certified PTs. To book a PT session, contact Member Services or ask at the club reception.
