# Raffles Medical Group — Overview

## About Raffles Medical Group

Raffles Medical Group (RMG) is one of Singapore's largest private integrated healthcare groups. Founded in 1976, the group has grown to operate more than 74 multi-disciplinary clinics across Singapore and an international presence in China, Japan, Cambodia, and Vietnam.

## Accreditation

Raffles Hospital is accredited by the Joint Commission International (JCI), a globally recognised benchmark for patient safety and quality care. This accreditation covers clinical care, patient rights, medication management, and infection control.

## Core Services

- General Practice (GP) and family medicine
- Specialist outpatient clinics (Cardiology, Orthopaedics, ENT, Gynaecology, Ophthalmology, Urology, and more)
- Raffles Hospital (585 North Bridge Road) — tertiary care facility with 24-hour A&E
- Executive health screening programmes
- Dental services (Raffles Dental)
- Traditional Chinese Medicine (Raffles Chinese Medicine)
- Teleconsultation (Raffles Connect)
- Pharmacy (Raffles Pharmacy)
- Radiology and imaging

## Network Scale

- 74+ clinics island-wide in Singapore
- Raffles Hospital (City Hall) — flagship tertiary care hospital
- Satellite clinics throughout heartland, commercial, and expat belt locations
- International offices: Shanghai, Chongqing, Beijing, Tokyo, Phnom Penh, Ho Chi Minh City

## Mission

To provide quality, accessible, and integrated healthcare to patients and communities in Singapore and the region.
