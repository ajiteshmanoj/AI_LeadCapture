# Raffles Medical Group — Services

## General Practice (GP)

Standard GP consultations for acute illnesses, chronic disease management, vaccinations, and minor procedures. Walk-ins are welcome; appointments are recommended to reduce waiting time.

## Specialist Clinics

Raffles Medical operates specialist outpatient clinics across multiple disciplines including:

- Cardiology
- Orthopaedics and Sports Medicine
- ENT (Ear, Nose & Throat)
- Gastroenterology
- Gynaecology and Obstetrics
- Ophthalmology (eye care)
- Urology
- Dermatology
- Oncology
- Neurology
- Respiratory Medicine
- Rheumatology

Specialist consultations typically require a referral letter from a GP. Patients can self-refer for some disciplines — please check with the clinic when booking.

## Executive Health Screening

Raffles Medical offers a range of health screening packages:

- Basic screening (blood panel, urine, BMI, vision)
- Comprehensive executive packages (cardiac stress test, CT scans, specialist reviews)
- Corporate wellness packages
- Cancer screening programmes (colonoscopy, mammography, PAP smear, PSA)

Most executive health screening packages at Raffles Medical Holland Village require fasting for at least 8 hours. Please confirm fasting requirements when booking.

Medisave can be used for approved health screening packages under the Medisave500 scheme.

## Dental

Raffles Dental provides general and specialist dental services including:

- General dentistry, scaling and polishing
- Dental implants
- Orthodontics (braces and Invisalign)
- Oral surgery

## Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM)

Raffles Chinese Medicine offers TCM consultations, acupuncture, and herbal medicine at selected locations.

## Teleconsultation (Raffles Connect)

Raffles Connect provides video or phone teleconsultations for:

- Follow-up reviews for stable chronic conditions
- Minor acute illnesses (rashes, conjunctivitis, upper respiratory tract infections)
- Prescription renewals for existing patients

Teleconsult is NOT suitable for emergencies or conditions requiring physical examination. Same-day slots may be available. Book via this chatbot or through the Raffles Connect app.

## Pharmacy

Raffles Pharmacy operates at most Raffles Medical clinic locations. Medications prescribed by RMG doctors can be dispensed on-site. Some over-the-counter products are available.

## Radiology and Imaging

Available at Raffles Hospital and selected clinics:

- X-ray
- Ultrasound
- MRI and CT scanning (at Raffles Hospital)
- Mammography
