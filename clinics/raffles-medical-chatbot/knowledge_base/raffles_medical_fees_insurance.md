# Raffles Medical Group — Fees and Insurance

## Standard Consultation Fees

| Consultation Type            | Fee (SGD)     | Notes                                  |
|------------------------------|---------------|----------------------------------------|
| GP Consultation              | $38           | Excludes medication and tests          |
| Specialist Consultation      | $150 – $300   | Depends on discipline and doctor       |
| Teleconsultation             | $20 – $38     | Platform fee may apply                 |
| Executive Health Screening   | $150 – $500+  | Package dependent                      |
| Dental (basic scaling)       | $60 – $120    | Varies by procedure                    |

All fees listed are before medication, investigations (blood tests, X-rays, etc.), and procedures. Final bills will reflect all items dispensed or performed during the visit.

## Medisave

Medisave can be used at Raffles Medical for:

- Approved specialist outpatient treatments (e.g. cardiology, oncology, dialysis)
- Approved day surgery and hospitalisation procedures at Raffles Hospital
- Approved health screening packages under the Medisave500 scheme (up to $500 per year)
- Vaccinations under the National Adult Immunisation Schedule (NAIS)

Medisave CANNOT be used for standard GP consultations.

Patients should bring their NRIC and Medisave/CPF card. Our staff can verify your Medisave eligibility at the point of registration.

## CHAS (Community Health Assist Scheme)

CHAS subsidies are available at selected Raffles Medical GP clinics for Singapore Citizens and PRs holding an orange, blue, or green CHAS card. Please present your CHAS card at registration. Subsidies apply to GP consultations and selected chronic disease management.

## Integrated Shield Plans (IP) / Private Insurance

Raffles Medical is on the panel of most major insurers and Integrated Shield Plan providers in Singapore, including:

- AIA, Prudential, Great Eastern, NTUC Income, AXA, Raffles Health Insurance

For outpatient specialist consultations and procedures, a Letter of Guarantee (LOG) from your insurer may be required before the appointment. Contact your insurer or our billing team to arrange this.

## Corporate / Company Billing

Raffles Medical accepts direct billing for employees covered by corporate medical benefits. Please bring your corporate medical card and staff ID. Not all clinics accept all corporate panels — please confirm when booking.

## Foreign Workers (FWL / MOM)

For work permit and S Pass holders, Raffles Medical provides medical examinations and consultation services. Employers may be billed directly under the company's corporate account.
