# Raffles Medical Group — Policies

## Appointment Booking

- Appointments can be booked via this chatbot, by phone, or by walking in during operating hours.
- Walk-in patients are welcome but may experience longer wait times during peak hours.
- For specialist consultations, a GP referral letter is generally required. Self-referrals are accepted for some specialties — please confirm when booking.
- Same-day appointments may be available for GP consultations; specialist appointments typically require advance booking.

## Cancellation and Rescheduling

- Please give at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule.
- Late cancellations (less than 24 hours) for health screening and specialist appointments may incur a cancellation fee.
- For health screening packages, no-shows may forfeit the appointment fee.
- To cancel or reschedule, call the clinic directly or notify via this chat.

## Referrals

- A referral letter from a GP (within or outside Raffles Medical) is generally required to see a specialist at reduced waiting time.
- Raffles Medical GPs can issue referrals to Raffles specialists on the same day.
- If you hold a referral from a restructured hospital or government polyclinic, please bring it to your appointment — it may affect subsidy eligibility.

## PDPA (Personal Data Protection)

Raffles Medical Group collects personal data (name, NRIC, date of birth, contact information, and health information) to provide healthcare services and manage appointments. Your data is:

- Used only for the stated purpose (appointment booking, clinical care, and billing)
- Not sold or shared with third parties except where required by law or with your consent
- Stored securely in compliance with Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA)

You have the right to access, correct, or withdraw consent to the use of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@rafflesmedical.com.

When using this chatbot, you acknowledge that your chat data may be used to manage your appointment. Type **STOP** at any time to opt out and end the conversation.

## NRIC Policy

For patient identity verification, we collect the last 4 characters of your NRIC (e.g. "123A"). We do NOT store or transmit full NRIC numbers through this chatbot. Please never share your full NRIC in the chat.

## Emergency Escalation

This chatbot is NOT an emergency service. For:

- Medical emergencies (chest pain, difficulty breathing, stroke, loss of consciousness): **Call 995 immediately.**
- Urgent but non-emergency concerns: ask to be connected to a nurse via this chat.
- General enquiries outside chat hours: call your preferred clinic directly.

Raffles Hospital 24-hour A&E: +65 6311 1111

## Patient Rights

Patients at Raffles Medical have the right to:

- Be treated with dignity and respect
- Receive clear information about their condition and treatment options
- Give or refuse consent for treatment
- Have their personal and medical information kept confidential
- Request a second opinion
- Provide feedback or raise complaints

## Feedback and Complaints

To provide feedback or raise a complaint, you can:

- Speak to the clinic manager at any Raffles Medical clinic
- Email patient.relations@rafflesmedical.com
- Call +65 6311 1111

All complaints are taken seriously and responded to within 7 working days.
